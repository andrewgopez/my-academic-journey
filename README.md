# My CEGEP Journey Timeline

## Project Description
A visual HTML/CSS timeline depicting my CEGEP journey of failure, reflection, and growth.  

This timeline accompanies the memoir I am writing, allowing me to **visually organize and reflect on key moments** in my academic and personal journey. It will also be **presented at the Expo** for graduates of the Arts, Literature, and Communication (ALC) program, where other students, teachers, and parents can follow my experiences in an engaging, chronological format, highlighting milestones, challenges, and moments of growth.

Each event includes:  
- A **date/semester** (e.g., Fall 2022, Winter 2024)  
- A **title** (short summary of the event)  
- A **description**, which can be a longer paragraph reflecting on the experience   

## Purpose
The timeline shows my journey, highlighting challenges, successes, and key moments  of my time in CEGEP.
