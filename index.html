My CEGEP Journey Timeline

Pre-CEGEP

Growing up, I always had a passion for technology and in the way computers worked. My dad works in the tech industry, and I would always watch him at work. After expressing my interest in learning how to code, he taught me how to design a website using HTML, the building block of web design. A few years later, during the COVID-19 pandemic, having nothing to do, my dad suggested I relearn HTML but also teach myself CSS and JavaScript, which are designed to give a website style and make it more interactive. Two years later, in 2022, this passion led me to apply to the Computer Science program at John Abbott College. Before I started, my dad suggested I also learn C#, the main programming language that would be taught, to give me a head start.
